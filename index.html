CARL MESIAS

Hello! My name is Carl Mesias, I am a self-taught Game Developer with experience in C/C++, C#, Unity, Unreal Engine, Gamemaker, Desktop Game Development, and Mobile Game Development, PSP (PlayStation) Game Development. I have a strong passion to create an enjoyable and unique gaming experience.

From a computer science major, to completing my nursing degree, I found my passion for game development when I was 15 years old creating games for the Sony PSP (PlayStation Portable). In pursuit of growing my passion I began building and learning how to create software in C/C++, and eventually branched out to C# with Unity, Blueprint with Unreal Engine and other game engines. I continually work on growing my skills to one day prepare myself in the game industry.

In the future, I would love to join a AAA Studio, or founding an Indie Studio filled with passionate gamers and game developers such as myself. In my free-time, I’m currently creating YouTube tutorials on how to create your first game. If you are interested in making your first game, send me a dm, I’ll send you a link. I’m also working on a game in Unreal Engine, stay tuned.
